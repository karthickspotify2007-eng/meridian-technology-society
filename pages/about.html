An independent society, built by practitioners

Meridian Technology Society is a nonprofit, member-governed organization advancing the practice of technology through open research, rigorous certification, and a global network of technical communities.

Our Mission

Advance technology practice through open research and shared standards

We believe the people building technology should also govern the standards it's held to. Meridian exists to make world-class research, credentialing, and professional community accessible to every technologist, everywhere — not just those inside well-resourced companies.

Open Research

Peer-reviewed publications, openly accessible to members, spanning eight technical disciplines.

Rigorous Standards

Vendor-neutral certifications developed by working practitioners, not marketing teams.

Global Community

210+ chapters and eight technical communities connecting members across 148 countries.

History & Milestones

Three decades of growth

1994

Founded by 40 practitioners

Meridian is founded as a small working group publishing an internal newsletter on distributed systems practice.

2003

First international conference

The inaugural Meridian Symposium draws 300 attendees across 12 countries.

2012

Certification program launches

The first vendor-neutral professional certification is introduced for site reliability engineering.

2019

Membership passes 250,000

Rapid growth in student chapters across Asia-Pacific and Latin America drives a decade-high growth rate.

2026

483,000+ members across 148 countries

Meridian launches its Applied AI Safety certification and opens its 40-year publication archive as open access.

Governance

Member-governed, transparently run

Meridian is governed by an elected Board of Governors, supported by standing committees for Publications, Conferences, Education, and Ethics. Board elections are held every two years and open to all Professional and Fellow members.

Financial statements, board meeting minutes, and bylaws are published annually to the membership.
